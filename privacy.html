Privacy Policy

This production-oriented draft is not yet in force. Legal owner, contact, jurisdiction, and final counsel review remain pending.

Overview

FingerSpa is designed with no account, no advertising, no third-party tracking, and no custom analytics service. We do not sell personal information. The app does not request access to Health, Contacts, Photos, Camera, Location, microphone, or cross-app tracking for its planned V1 features.

Information stored on your device

The app may store preferences, favorites, session history, and a hand-layout calibration profile locally on your iPhone. Calibration represents interaction layout preferences; it is not used to identify you and is not treated as a medical record.

A device-local Keychain record tracks whether the single complimentary session has reached its qualified-use threshold. The record is monotonic: once consumed, it does not reset through normal app use.

Minimal iCloud Key-Value Store record

If iCloud Key-Value Store is available, the app may mirror only a schema version, a consumed/not-consumed value, and a consumed timestamp for the complimentary session. Hand-calibration geometry, touch samples, favorites, and session history are not included in this mirror. The app does not use CloudKit or a FingerSpa-operated backend.

Purchases

Lifetime Unlock purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process purchase, account, payment, tax, and transaction information under Apple’s own terms and privacy policies. FingerSpa uses Apple-verified entitlement information to decide whether unlocked features are available.

Retention and deletion

Local settings and history remain until changed, cleared through an available in-app control, or removed with the app, subject to normal iOS and Keychain behavior. The complimentary-session state may remain in Keychain or the minimal iCloud KVS mirror to prevent the one-time benefit from moving backward. StoreKit purchase records are managed by Apple.

Children and sensitive uses

FingerSpa is a general lifestyle experience, not a medical service, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency tool. The app is not designed to collect information from children or intentionally collect sensitive personal data.
