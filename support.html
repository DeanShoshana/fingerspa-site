Support

Pre-release foundation. Public support contact will be activated before release.

Device requirement

FingerSpa requires a supported physical iPhone with Core Haptics capability and a supported iOS version. Simulator results cannot reproduce or validate the physical sensation.

Comfort or device movement

Stop the session immediately if you are uncomfortable, the phone moves unexpectedly, or you hear concerning mechanical noise. Use a stable surface and avoid unsafe situations. FingerSpa is not a medical service or treatment.

Sound

Every session is designed to remain usable without sound. Use the active-session mute control to change sound immediately, or Settings to change the default for future sessions.

Purchases and restoration

Lifetime Unlock is a one-time In-App Purchase, not a subscription. Use Restore Purchase in Settings when reinstalling or using another eligible device with the same Apple Account. Apple manages billing and refund requests.

Contact

Support email pending. When contacting support after launch, include the iPhone model, iOS version, app version, whether a case is fitted, and concise steps to reproduce the issue. Do not send passwords, MFA codes, payment-card details, private keys, or other secrets.
